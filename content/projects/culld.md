title: "CULLD"
status: "WORK IN PROGRESS"
---

Culld is a recruiting platform built for agent-native workflows. It is designed around the idea that AI systems should be able to search structured talent data, evaluate fit, and support hiring workflows in a more direct way than traditional professional networks.

The project combines profile ingestion, filtering, verification, and matching into a system intended for recruiters, candidates, and companies.
