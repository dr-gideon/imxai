About

Grounded in systems. Built in collaboration.

My background is in SAP Business One, business systems, reporting, and data workflows, where I spent years turning operational needs into working solutions for users and stakeholders.

That work taught me how to think in systems, not features. I learned how processes break, where teams get blocked, and what useful software actually has to do once it leaves the whiteboard.

IM x AI reflects how I work now: human judgment, direction, and taste, strengthened by serious collaboration with AI during research, prototyping, implementation, and iteration.

I am most interested in work that sits close to infrastructure, users, and operational reality: the layer where product thinking, technical depth, and practical automation meet.
